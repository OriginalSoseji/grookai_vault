'''American Gothic''' is the name of the deck used by Ian Whiton, who made it to the Semifinals in the Senior Division in the [[2013 World Championships]]. It is one of the four {{TCGMerch|Black & White|Series|2013 World Championships Decks}}, released on November 4, 2013. Each Deck comes with a Worlds 2013 themed Deckbox, a booklet about the championships, a non-tournament-legal print of the sixty card deck, and the 2013 World Championships Pin.

==Description==
<i>Ian Whiton's devastating American Gothic deck was a masterpiece of control for him and a masterpiece of frustration for everyone else!  Unlucky opponents found their Active Pokémon rapidly locked in place and unable to benefit from Item cards as focus-shifting attacks were used to rain damage onto the Bench through a mighty combination of Accelgor, Gothitelle, Dusknoir, and Mew-EX.  With nowhere to run and a severely compromised arsenal, foes fell by the wayside at impressive speed.</i>
