'''Darkrai Deck''' is the name of the deck used by [[Jason Klaczynski]], who won the Masters Division in the [[2013 World Championships]]. It is one of the four {{TCGMerch|Black & White|Series|2013 World Championships Decks}}, released on November 4, 2013. Each Deck comes with a Worlds 2013 themed Deckbox, a booklet about the championships, a non-tournament-legal print of the sixty card deck, and the 2013 World Championships Pin.

==Description==
<i>Three-time Pokémon TCG World Champion Jason Klaczynski and his Darkrai deck rolled all the way to the 2013 Masters title with a ferocious combination of speed and versatility. Darkrai-EX confounded opponents with cheap attacks and extreme mobility, while Sableye's excellent card-finding talents ensured that the perfect strategic play was never far away. The result? A fluid, flexible aggressive deck: nightmarish to face in battle!</i>
