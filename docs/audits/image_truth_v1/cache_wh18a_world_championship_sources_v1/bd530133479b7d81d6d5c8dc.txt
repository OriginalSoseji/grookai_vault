'''Pesadelo Prism''' is the name of the deck used by Igor Costa, the Masters Division Champion in the [[2012 World Championships]]. It is one of the four {{TCGMerch|Black & White|Series|2012 World Championships Decks}}, released in November 2012. Each deck comes packaged with a 2012 World Championships Booklet, Deck Box, and Special Pin. Each card features a silver border, Costa's signature, and limited edition 2012 World Championships back. None of the cards are tournament legal.

==Description==
''World Champion Igor Costa's Pesadelo Prism deck focuses on card-drawing power and speeding up the flow of Energy to quickly power up Darkrai-EX and Mewtwo-EX! Drawing is helped by cards such as Professor Oak's New Theory and N, while Dark Patch accelerates Energy acquisition and Shaymin moves it around to set up punishing attacks. This was a dream deck for the Champion and will be a nightmare for everyone else!''
