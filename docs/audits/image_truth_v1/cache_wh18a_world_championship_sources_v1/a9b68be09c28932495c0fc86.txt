'''Ultimate Team Plasma''' is the name of the deck used by Yugo Sato, who was second in the Junior Division in the [[2013 World Championships]]. It is one of the four {{TCGMerch|Black & White|Series|2013 World Championships Decks}}, released on November 4, 2013. Each Deck comes with a Worlds 2013 themed Deckbox, a booklet about the championships, a non-tournament-legal print of the sixty card deck, and the 2013 World Championships Pin.

==Description==
''This deck focusses on getting the most out of Kyurem's Blizzard Burn attack. Thundurus EX helps power it up quickly and Deoxys EX gives it extra punch. When combined with Hypnotoxic Laser and Virbank City Gym, even EXs can be knocked out in one turn.''
