'''Twinboar''' is the name of the deck used by David Cohen, the Masters Champion in the [[2011 World Championships]]. The deck is a specific build of the {{TCG|MagneBoar}} archetype that focuses on {{p|Reshiram}}, {{p|Magnezone}} and {{p|Emboar}}. The strategy of the deck is to use {{TCG ID|Black & White|Reshiram|26}}'s {{m|Blue Flare}} attack each turn, and reattaching the {{TCG|Fire Energy}} by means of {{TCG ID|Black & White|Emboar|20}}'s Inferno Fandango {{TCG|Ability}}, with {{TCG ID|Triumphant|Magnezone|96}} providing draw support through its {{TCG|Poké-Power}}, Magnetic Draw.

Twinboar is one of the four {{TCGMerch|Black & White|Series|2011 World Championships Decks}} released in October 2011. With the deck came a 2011 World Championships Deck Box, and a Champions Booklet.

==Description==
''With Cleffa in place to stoke the flames and the {{TCG|Trainer card}} {{TCG ID|Triumphant|Twins|89}} to get you just what you need when you need it, World Champion David Cohen uses a combination of {{TCG ID|Black & White|Emboar|20}} and {{TCG ID|Black & White|Reshiram|26}} to blaze the trail to his title. In an added flash of inspiration, his addition of {{TCG ID|Undaunted|Rayquaza & Deoxys LEGEND|89}} can surprise your opponent and earn you two {{TCG|Prize card}}s to win the game in a blaze of glory!''
