Abyss Eye (Japanese: アビスアイ Abyss Eye) is the name given to the fifth expansion of cards released during the Japanese Pokémon Trading Card Game's MEGA Series. This set is based on the Mega Dimension DLC of the video game Pokémon Legends: Z-A and features Mega Darkrai as the central Pokémon.

Information

Abyss Eye is the first set to be based on the Mega Dimension DLC for Pokémon Legends: Z-A, and continues to introduce Mega Evolution Pokémon ex and Trainer cards from the video game. The set primarily focuses on Mega Darkrai ex as the central Pokémon.

Abyss Eye was released in Japan on May 22, 2026, followed by localized releases in Taiwan and Hong Kong on June 5, 2026, and in South Korea on June 26, 2026. Each booster pack contains 5 cards. Each booster box contains 30 packs. Cards released in this set are included in the English Pitch Black expansion.

Abyss Eye was first revealed to the public on April 17, 2026, the date of a new moon, which is said to be when Darkrai is active.
