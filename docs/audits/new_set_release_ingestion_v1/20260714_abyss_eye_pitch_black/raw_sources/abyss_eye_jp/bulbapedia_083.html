Armarouge (Japanese: グレンアルマ Gurenarma) is a Fire-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the Pitch Black expansion.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card and a Full Art illustration rare card in the Pitch Black expansion, first released in the Japanese Abyss Eye expansion. The Regular print features artwork by Jiro Sasumo, while the Full Art illustration rare print features artwork by Iwamoto05.

A Holofoil version of the Regular print with a "What's Your Favorite?" logo in the bottom right corner of the artwork was given away with eligible Pokémon Trading Card Game purchases of $15 or more at participating retailers in various countries, to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the Pokémon franchise. Participating retailers include Best Buy in the United States and JB Hi-Fi in New Zealand.[1]
