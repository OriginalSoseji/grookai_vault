Fomantis (Japanese: カリキリ Karikiri) is a Grass-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Pitch Black expansion.

Pokédex entry
Many Trainers give their Fomantis their own flowerpots so they can sunbathe in peace and quiet.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card and a Full Art illustration rare card in the Pitch Black expansion, first released in the Japanese Abyss Eye expansion. The Regular print features artwork by nisimono, while the Full Art illustration rare print features artwork by Jiro Sasumo.

Trivia

Origin

Reckless Charge is an attack that first appeared on Ash's Phanpy from the Movie Commemoration VS Pack: Aura's Lucario. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Violet.
