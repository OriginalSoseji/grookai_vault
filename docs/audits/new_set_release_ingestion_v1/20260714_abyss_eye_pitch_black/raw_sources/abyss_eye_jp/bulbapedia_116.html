Gladion's Final Battle (Japanese: グラジオの決戦 Glazio's Decisive Battle) is a Supporter card. It is part of the Pitch Black expansion.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art ultra rare card, and a special illustration rare card in the Pitch Black expansion, first released in the Japanese Abyss Eye expansion. The Regular and Full Art ultra rare prints feature artwork by akagi, while the special illustration rare print features artwork by DOM.

Trivia

The special illustration rare print of this card forms a combined illustration with the illustration rare print of Silvally from the same set.

Origin

Gladion is an enforcer for Team Skull in the Pokémon games.
